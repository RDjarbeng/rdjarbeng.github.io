The landscape of software engineering has shifted dramatically over the past decade. Frontend development now involves intricate state management, complex build pipelines, and endless framework iterations that often surpass the architectural burdens once reserved for backend systems. Developers must navigate a dense ecosystem of tools that prioritize modularity and performance but frequently sacrifice simplicity in the process. This shift brings a new set of challenges regarding maintenance and long term scalability.
