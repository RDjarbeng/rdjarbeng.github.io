The  precarious stack of modern digital infrastructure.


A satirical diagram showing the precarious stack of modern digital infrastructure. At the top, a chaotic, towering pile of blocks representing software, AI, and cloud providers (like AWS and Cloudflare) balances precariously on a single tiny block labeled "Unpaid Opensource Developers." This rests on a layer of consumer hardware brands (Apple, Lenovo) and chipmakers (Intel, AMD). The entire upper structure balances on a single, tiny pillar labeled "Taiwan." Beneath Taiwan is a deep, sturdy foundation of semiconductor foundries (TSMC, Samsung), manufacturing equipment companies (ASML, Applied Materials), and chip design firms (ARM, Synopsys). The absolute bottom bedrock supporting everything consists of two images: a United States one-hundred-dollar bill and a US Navy aircraft carrier strike group.
