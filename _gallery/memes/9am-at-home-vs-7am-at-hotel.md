9am at home vs 7am at hotel

The difference that travel makes

**Additional comments:**

There is a strange and undeniable shift in motivation when travel is involved. At home, the morning is often a slow crawl toward reality. A 9am wake up time feels like a luxury that must be protected at all costs. The comfort of a familiar bed makes it incredibly difficult to find any reason to leave the warmth of the blankets. Life moves at a steady pace, and the snooze button is a common companion.

Everything changes the moment a hotel room enters the equation. Even if the vacation is meant for relaxation, the lure of the hotel breakfast buffet provides a powerful incentive to rise before the sun. Being up at 7am feels like a productive mission rather than a chore. The sight of fresh fruit, warm pastries, and endless coffee turns an early morning into a highlight of the day. Travel provides a unique energy that makes even the earliest hours feel like an adventure worth waking up for.
