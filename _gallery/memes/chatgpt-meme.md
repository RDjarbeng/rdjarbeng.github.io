**Additional comments:**

The landscape of software development has shifted dramatically with the emergence of artificial intelligence tools. Developers currently favor the rapid, conversational assistance provided by ChatGPT for troubleshooting code and generating solutions. This shift has pushed traditional pillars of the programming community, such as Stack Overflow, into the background as coders prioritize speed over the structured community verification found on legacy platforms. 

Meanwhile, official documentation sits at the bottom of the ocean. Often viewed as too dense or time consuming to navigate, comprehensive manuals and technical guides are frequently neglected by developers in favor of immediate, albeit sometimes hallucinated, answers from AI models. While efficiency is a major priority in modern engineering, relying solely on automated shortcuts often leads to a deeper misunderstanding of the fundamental frameworks and libraries being used. Documentation remains the single most accurate source of truth for any technology, even if it is currently ignored by the majority of the community.
