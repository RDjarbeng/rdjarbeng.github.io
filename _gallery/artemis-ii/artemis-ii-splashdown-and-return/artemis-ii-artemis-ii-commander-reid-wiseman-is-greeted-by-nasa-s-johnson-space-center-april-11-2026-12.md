(April 11, 2026) - Artemis II Commander Reid Wiseman is greeted by NASA's Johnson Space Center Director Vanessa Wyche on his return home to Houston, at Ellington Airport on Saturday, April 11, 2026, following a 10-day trip around the Moon and back. Wiseman and his three fellow crew members launched April 1, 2026, from NASA’s Kennedy Space Center in Florida, and splashed down off the coast of California on Friday, April 10, 2026.
