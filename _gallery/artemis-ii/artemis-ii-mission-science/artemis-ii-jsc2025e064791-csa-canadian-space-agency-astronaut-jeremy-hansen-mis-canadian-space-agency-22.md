 CSA (Canadian Space Agency) astronaut Jeremy Hansen, mission specialist for NASA’s Artemis II mission, foreground, and NASA astronaut and Artemis II commander, Reid Wiseman, participate in crew lunar observations training in the Orion mockup at NASA's Johnson Space Center in Houston. The Artemis II crew will be the first people in more than 50 years to set eyes on the far side of the Moon, depending on illumination conditions. They will document their observations through photographs, audio recordings, and more to inform our understanding of the Moon, and share their experience of being far from Earth.
