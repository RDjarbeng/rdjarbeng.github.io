Cover image for The Imperfections of Machine Learning

**Additional comments:**

Machine learning models often produce results that highlight the gap between digital perception and human reality. In this demonstration, an ML5 video analysis incorrectly identifies a common power adapter as a cell phone. This error serves as a perfect example of the limitations inherent in object detection technology. Even with a high confidence score of over 94 percent, the algorithm fails to distinguish between the adapter and the intended device. These technical blunders are essential for understanding how computer vision models interpret the world. Every misidentified object provides valuable insight into the training data biases and the complexities of real world image processing.
