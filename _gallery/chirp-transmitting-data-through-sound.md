**Additional comments:**

The landscape of digital communication is evolving through the innovative application of audio frequency modulation. This interface for the Chirp project demonstrates the fascinating process of encoding information into sound waves. By converting data into specific frequencies, systems can facilitate a seamless bridge between modern computing environments and acoustic signals. The dashboard showcases active transmission states, complete with transmission speed, character counts, and precise frequency markers for spaces, numbers, and letters. Witnessing the transition from a standard terminal input to an encoded audio signal provides a glimpse into the mechanics of data transfer. This project represents a compelling fusion of people and machines, proving that even simple audio signals possess the power to carry complex, meaningful information across the digital void.
