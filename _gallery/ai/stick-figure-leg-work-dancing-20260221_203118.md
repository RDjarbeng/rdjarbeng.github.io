Stick figure trying to do leg-work dancing created by Gemini nano banana pro. This was one of the shots that is hard to generate because if anyone has seen leg-work dancing for afrobeats before you find that it's a bit hard to describe and harder to draw. I tried specifying dancing on one leg as well as other prompts to get this final result which is good enough. I put this into Google veo 3 to make the video; I will include a link for that below.
