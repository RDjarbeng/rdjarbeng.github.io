**Additional comments:**

The Rwanda Utilities Regulatory Authority has officially issued a notification regarding the revision of electricity end user tariffs. This update follows the established legal framework provided by Law number 09 of 2013, which mandates the regulatory oversight of utility pricing. These adjustments are designed to reflect current economic conditions and ensure the sustainable provision of power services across the nation. Residents and business owners should take note that these new rates will come into full effect starting on 01 October 2025. It is essential for all stakeholders to review the official documentation to understand how these modifications may impact their energy costs. Proper awareness of these regulatory changes ensures compliance and effective financial planning for energy consumption in the coming year.
