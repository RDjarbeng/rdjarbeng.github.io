A breathtaking view of the sprawling city of Kigali, famously known as the heart of the "Land of a Thousand Hills." This landscape photograph captures a dramatic moment where bright crepuscular rays—sunlight breaking through the heavy cloud cover—illuminate the distant rolling hills and vibrant cityscape below. 

From this elevated vantage point, the sheer scale and natural beauty of Rwanda's capital city are on full display, perfectly blending urban life with its stunning geographical backdrop. The silhouette of the distant mountains provides a beautiful contrast to the bright light piercing through the sky.
