Cover image for What is TechRxiv?

TechRxiv serves as a vital hub for the global research community. This open, moderated preprint server specializes in unpublished work across engineering, computer science, and related technology fields. By providing a platform for early dissemination, it allows researchers to share their findings rapidly before formal peer review. The statistics speak for themselves, as the platform has already garnered over ten million views and millions of downloads. It effectively bridges the gap between initial discovery and final publication, fostering innovation by making knowledge accessible to engineers and scientists worldwide. Using TechRxiv ensures that your technical breakthroughs reach a broad audience immediately, while maintaining the rigor of a moderated scholarly environment. It is an essential resource for anyone looking to stay at the cutting edge of technological advancements.
