The modern development landscape is undergoing a massive transformation. Developers are increasingly turning to generative AI tools like ChatGPT for instant code snippets, debugging, and architectural advice. This convenience has fundamentally shifted reliance away from traditional community forums such as Stack Overflow, which now faces a decline in active engagement as users prioritize speed and conversational problem solving. While this transition boosts productivity for many engineers, it has also caused a complete abandonment of official project documentation. These technical manuals and specifications are essentially forgotten, left to decay on the ocean floor of the internet while the shiny new tools get all the attention. This trend highlights a growing preference for quick, synthesized answers over deep, foundational study of how software actually functions.
