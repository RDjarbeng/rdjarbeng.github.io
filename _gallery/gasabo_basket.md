**Additional comments:**

Kigali is a city of rapid transformation and striking architecture, but few sights capture the imagination quite like the Gasabo District headquarters. Perched atop this modern government building sits a massive, traditional woven basket structure that immediately draws the eye. This unique architectural feature serves as a proud nod to Rwandan heritage, celebrating the intricate craftsmanship of traditional basketry that has long been a staple of local culture. By blending contemporary office design with deeply rooted cultural symbolism, the building stands as a testament to the nation's ability to honor the past while building toward the future. It creates a bold visual contrast against the blue sky and serves as a primary landmark for anyone navigating the bustling Gasabo area.
