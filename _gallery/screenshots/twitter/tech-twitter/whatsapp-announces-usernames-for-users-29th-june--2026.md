Whatsapp announces Usernames for Users-29th June, 2026

WhatsApp has announced that usernames will be rolling out to users. The announcement was made via their official Twitter account.

This feature is not yet available for every user but is rolling out gradually. It also requires the latest version of WhatsApp

**Additional comments:**

WhatsApp is revolutionizing digital privacy with the introduction of unique usernames. This feature allows users to connect with others without the need to disclose their personal phone numbers. By moving away from a strictly phone number based identity, WhatsApp empowers users to maintain better control over their private data and personal information. This update represents a significant step forward in messaging security and user convenience.

To secure your preferred handle, navigate to the settings menu, select the account tab, and then choose the username option. This process requires the most recent version of the application to function correctly. Users are encouraged to reserve their desired usernames early to ensure their chosen identity is captured before the full global rollout concludes later this year. This proactive approach ensures that your presence on the platform remains both professional and private.
