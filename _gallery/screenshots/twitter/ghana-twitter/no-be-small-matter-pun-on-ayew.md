No be small matter-Pun on Ayew

To understand this joke you have to know the typical response to the question when a school child in Ghana is asked

> What is matter?

Every Ghanaian school child goes:

> Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space.

Understanding that you see that the tweet has some underappreciated context:

> Ayew just has weight and occupies space.

Then the quote, 'no be small matter oo' is just a play on words.

**Additional comments:**

In the world of physics, matter is defined by its ability to possess mass and occupy physical space. This scientific concept finds a humorous intersection with the celebrity world in a clever play on words involving the name Ayew.
