The 3 buckets Career strategy for people with too many interests

From YouTube

**Additional comments:**

Managing multiple passions often feels overwhelming, but the three buckets career strategy offers a clear path forward. You can organize your professional life by assigning different roles to distinct categories. The first bucket is for the money maker, which focuses on stable income streams that fund your lifestyle. The second bucket contains the soul stuff, representing work that provides personal fulfillment and aligns with your deepest values. Finally, the third bucket is for the curiosity shelf, which serves as a sandbox for experimental projects and new skills that keep you intellectually engaged. This framework prevents burnout by acknowledging that one single job does not need to satisfy every ambition. Implementing this method creates balance and provides the freedom to explore diverse interests without sacrificing financial security.
