# A Timeless Classic: "You'll Be in My Heart"

When it comes to unforgettable animated movie soundtracks, few can rival the emotional depth and universal appeal of Disney's 1999 classic, *Tarzan*. At the heart of this iconic soundtrack is the moving lullaby and pop anthem, **"You'll Be in My Heart,"** written and performed by the legendary **Phil Collins**.

## The Heart of the Song

This video, which has amassed over 71 million views, features the complete original audio of the beloved song. The track seamlessly blends a tender, protective lullaby with a powerful, driving rhythm—a signature of Phil Collins' musical style. 

The lyrics, beginning with the comforting lines *"Come stop your crying, it will be all right,"* perfectly encapsulate the unconditional love and protective bond between mother and child. Within the context of the film, it represents a mother gorilla adopting an orphaned human baby, but the words transcend the movie's storyline to become a universal anthem of love, reassurance, and found family. 

Whether you are revisiting it for a dose of 90s nostalgia or experiencing its heartfelt message for the first time, "You'll Be in My Heart" remains a powerful testament to the enduring magic of Disney's musical storytelling.
