Many found this song from Apostle Joshua Selman after he used it in a service and had to go search for it. According to Google the song was released in 2022 but this video was premiered on YouTube July, 3rd 2025.

The song starts off with a short intro and a vocal from Kaestrings himself and his guitar. The set is identical to the one used for his hit song Rahama. Honestly don't know which of these hits came first. Anyway back to the song. There's a short instrumental, then the choir and audience joins with 'Your presence is my one desire...'  Then we go into the Chorus,

> You’re all I want
> You’re all I need
> I wanna hold You close to me
> You’re all I want
> You’re all I need
> To see Your Kingdom’s my heart cry

After the chorus, we come back to the verse with even more energy but with a slight change, instead of presence we want GLORY.

> Your Glory is my one desire

Sublime! The instrumental also backs the song nicely and we continue in blissful melody.
After the verse, we go for the chorus. And then at the end Kaestrings vocalises the melody with

> A yaa ya ya ya ya yaaa
>  and
> Oh, oh ooh oooh, ooh

Then the instrumentals take over while Kaestrings speaks.  Then a slowed version of the chorus with the drummer in marching band pace on the snare drum. Eventually the snare drum ceases to give way to just strings and the sound of the choir and studio audience.

> You’re all I want
> You’re all I need
> I wanna hold You close to me
> You’re all I want
> You’re all I need
> To see Your Kingdom’s my heart cry
> The song ends gently with Kaestrings saying,
> Your presence is my one desire
