The speech in the video is given by the character **Madara Uchiha** from the anime and manga series _Naruto_.

### **Who is Speaking?**

The speaker is Madara Uchiha, the legendary leader of the Uchiha clan. He is one of the most powerful shinobi in history and served as a major antagonist in the series.

### **To Whom is He Speaking?**

He is speaking to a young **Obito Uchiha**. At this point in the story, Obito was thought to have died during a mission but was secretly saved and nursed back to health by an elderly Madara.

### **Where Are They?**

The conversation takes place in an underground hideout known as **Mountains' Graveyard**. Madara was living there in secret, sustained by the Gedo Statue.

### **What is the Context?**

This scene occurs during the Third Shinobi World War. Madara is using this philosophical speech to manipulate Obito and win him over to his cause.

- **Philosophical Nihilism:** Madara explains his belief that the world is a cycle of pain where light cannot exist without shadows, and peace cannot exist without war.
- **The Goal:** He wants to "sever the fate of this world" by creating a dream world of only victors, peace, and love. This sets the stage for the "Eye of the Moon Plan" (Infinite Tsukuyomi), which aims to trap the entire world in a permanent, peaceful genjutsu (illusion).
- **Legacy:** In the video, Madara identifies himself as the "Ghost of the Uchiha," passing his ideology and name onto Obito so that the plan can continue after Madara's natural death.
