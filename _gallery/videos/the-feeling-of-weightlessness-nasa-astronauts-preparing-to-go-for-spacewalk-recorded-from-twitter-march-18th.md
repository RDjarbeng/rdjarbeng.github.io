Here on March 18  watch astronauts [@Astro_Jessica](https://x.com/Astro_Jessica) and [@Astro_ChrisW](https://x.com/Astro_ChrisW) prepare to go on a spacewalk! It'll be Williams' first and Meir's fourth. The duo make preparations to install solar arrays. The event was livestreamed on X/Twitter. 

## Viewing Experience

The astronauts are weightless and are floating in the space station. It's cool seeing their tools floating and wires just suspended in the ear without drooping. The female astronauts pony tail is suspended almost as if she's in water giving a cool effect that comes with weightlessness. A third astronaut joined them shortly before this video and took a photo with his Ipad and it was suspended shortly in the air. One thing I notice is that the pace of movement seems natural and not slowed like you see in the hollywood movies when someone is weightless.
