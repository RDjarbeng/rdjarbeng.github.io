**Additional comments:**

Designing a functional living space requires precision, spatial awareness, and a keen eye for dimensions. In a bold experiment to test the limits of current technology, I tasked Gemini Pro with generating a perfect room layout. The process involved rigorous iterative design where the AI had to reconcile specific furniture measurements with restricted floor space. While initial attempts resulted in impractical arrangements, the AI eventually demonstrated significant growth in spatial reasoning and dimension accuracy. This project highlights the potential for artificial intelligence to serve as a supportive tool in interior design tasks by quickly visualizing complex floor plans. Using advanced models like Gemini Pro allows for rapid prototyping of furniture placement before moving a single heavy item. My findings reveal that while human expertise remains irreplaceable for nuanced aesthetic choices, AI is becoming a powerful partner for tackling the logistical puzzles of room design.
