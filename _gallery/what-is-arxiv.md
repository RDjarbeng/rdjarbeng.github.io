Cover image for What is arXiv?

arXiv serves as a cornerstone for the global research community by providing an open access repository for nearly 2.4 million scholarly articles. This platform democratizes information by allowing researchers in fields like physics, mathematics, and computer science to share their findings with the public before formal peer review. Its extensive archive covers diverse topics ranging from astrophysics and quantum physics to artificial intelligence and computational geometry. Utilizing this service ensures that cutting edge discoveries reach the scientific community instantly without the traditional barriers of paywalls. Scholars and enthusiasts alike rely on arXiv to stay updated with the latest breakthroughs in their respective disciplines through a user friendly search and browse interface.
