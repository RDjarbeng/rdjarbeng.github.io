King Promise, a Ghanaian musician launched a banger in July, 2023

# King Promise - Terminator feat. Young Jonn

If you thought from the title that this was from the Arnold Schwarzenegger movie that came out in 1984 then you are wrong. There is a reference to the terminator movie at the start with some kinda low-budget CGI with sounds of electricity cackling. The scene shows King Promise in dark shades and his regular bald head levitating in some kind of sci-fi futuristic looking lab. This scene runs for about 8 seconds before we get back to the song. Back to reality. Probably not the terminator you were looking for if you were searching for the movie.
