Modern AI development requires selecting the right model for specific requirements. This guide outlines the best choices for various technical tasks based on current availability.

## Reasoning and Logic

For complex logical deductions and high level reasoning, GPT OSS 120B is the primary choice. It handles multi step problems with high precision. Qwen 3 32B offers a balanced alternative for efficient logical processing on smaller resource footprints.

## Function Calling and Tool Use

Llama 4 Scout excels at executing external tools and maintaining consistent function calling structures. It integrates seamlessly into automated workflows. Qwen 3 32B remains a strong contender for simpler tool interactions in reasoning heavy contexts.

## Audio Processing

Orpheus English and Orpheus Arabic Saudi provide high fidelity text to speech output. They are essential for accessible interfaces and voice applications. For speech to text, Whisper Large v3 and its Turbo variant deliver industry leading transcription accuracy.

## Multilingual Support

Llama 3.3 70B is highly effective for large scale multilingual text generation. It supports diverse languages with cultural nuance. GPT OSS 120B and Llama 4 Scout also provide robust multilingual capabilities across various modalities.

## Vision and Multimodal Tasks

Llama 4 Scout serves as the go to model for vision related tasks. It interprets visual data and converts it into actionable text or structured logic. This versatility makes it ideal for mixed media environments.
