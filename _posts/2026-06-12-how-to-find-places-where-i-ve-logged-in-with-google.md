To see all the apps, websites, and services where you used "Log in with Google," visit your [Google Account Connection](https://myaccount.google.com/connections) page==. [[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G3vos_Ffuas)]

Here is how to check it on your phone or computer: [[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G3vos_Ffuas)]

1. Go to your [Google Account Dashboard](https://myaccount.google.com/).
2. Click the **Security** tab on the left side of your screen.
3. Scroll down to **Your connections to third-party apps & services**.
4. Tap **See all connections** to view the full list. [[1](https://it.loyno.edu/how-do-i-see-what-devices-are-currently-signed-your-google-account), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G3vos_Ffuas)]

From this list, you can click on any app to see what personal data it has access to. If you do not use an app anymore, you can safely remove its access to your Google account.
